About The Band

Marwood's Fall is a hard rock band from

the greater Chicago area. The band started in 2009 when brothers Matthew (guitar) and David (vocals) Danaher were introduced to drummer John Eggan through a college friend. The three bonded quickly over shared influences and began writing music and developing their sound. After some early line up changes and recording frustrations, the trio were eventually able to release their first EP, Restitution, in 2013, at which point Ben Peterson (bass) joined the group and they hit the stage playing local Chicago clubs, suburban venues, and a few shows around the midwest.

After performing at some of Chicago's most revered rock venues, including the House of Blues and the iconic Metro, the band spent the latter part of 2015 recording their sophomore EP, Judgement Is Coming, at Always Be Genius studios with producer Seth Henderson. The EP was released in May of 2016 and represents a huge step forward in both writing and performance quality. Judgement explores a range of themes from the driving single "Ararat", an apocalyptic song with overtones of Tool and the Deftones to the hopeful and poignant finale, "Nero", which sounds more like Breaking Benjamin and gives a subtle nod to Queen. Marwood's Fall is back performing around Chicago and seeking opportunities to share their music with a wider audience.
